About GRL

Innovative logistics company delivering excellence through owned fleet and customer-centric solutions

Our Story

GRL is an innovative, customer-centric logistics company founded in 2025, headquartered in Ayarakunnam.

As a sister concern of GRT (founded in 2016 with 13 to 14 feet container trailers), we specialize in end-to-end supply chain solutions designed to meet the dynamic needs of modern businesses worldwide.

What sets us apart: Unlike most logistics companies that rely on third-party vehicles, GRL owns and operates a dedicated fleet of 16 vehicles. This gives us complete control over delivery quality, timing, and customer service standards.
